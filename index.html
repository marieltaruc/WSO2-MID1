About Moana

Moana is the spirited and determined protagonist of the film "Moana." She is a young Polynesian girl who possesses a deep connection to the ocean and a longing for adventure beyond the confines of her island, Motunui.

Moana's Characteristics

  • She is sea-loving
  • She is strong-willed
  • She is practically fearless
